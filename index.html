World of Warcraft Survey

This is a study on player experience in World of Warcraft. The main focus is on active World of Warcraft: Shadowlands (or "retail") players. The questionnaire should take around 10 minutes to complete.

The information collected in this form will be published and discussed in a project report, but the individual information there will be anonymous. The participation is voluntary, and you may withdraw at any point. In case of withdrawal, I am obliged to destroy your provided data once I am informed about the withdrawal. Should you have any questions, I can be contacted via e-mail: dpered18@student.aau.dk

Share your valuable opinion and answer the survey.
